Introduction

Pawfect Match is a web application developed by Jade and Olivia to support animal shelters by increasing adoption visibility for pets that have remained in care the longest. The platform is designed to prioritize these long-term residents, placing them at the forefront of the site to help them gain the attention they often lack.

By focusing on overlooked animals, Pawfect Match aims to promote more compassionate and equitable adoptions. In addition to serving this mission, the project also functions as a practical application of full-stack web development, combining both design and functionality in support of a meaningful cause.

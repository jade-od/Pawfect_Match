Our Mission

At Pawfect Match, our mission is to bring attention to the animals who are too often overlooked, the ones who have waited the longest for a chance at a forever home. Every shelter pet deserves love, safety, and companionship, but many of them remain unnoticed simply because they’ve been there longer. Our platform is designed to challenge that pattern by placing these long-term residents at the forefront of the adoption process.

We believe visibility is a powerful tool. By giving priority placement to animals with the longest shelter stays, we hope to encourage adopters to consider pets they may not have noticed otherwise. These animals have just as much love to give, and often, even more gratitude to show. Through thoughtful design and purposeful organization, Pawfect Match aims to create a more compassionate and inclusive adoption experience.

This project also represents our commitment to learning and applying real-world development skills in a way that creates impact. By integrating both front-end and back-end technologies, we have built a platform that not only works, but also works for good. Through this process, we have explored how design, data, and empathy can come together to support an important cause.

Ultimately, Pawfect Match exists to remind people that every animal has a story worth hearing. We hope our platform helps bridge the gap between shelters and potential adopters, and that it inspires more people to consider giving a second chance to a pet who has been waiting just a little longer for theirs.

Meet the Creators

Jade

I co-created Pawfect Match as a way to help shine a light on shelter pets who are often overlooked, especially the ones who’ve been waiting the longest. I’ve always had a deep love for rescue animals and have adopted two cats from shelters. My current companions, Cupid and Apollo, are both rescues, just like the two cats I had before them. One thing that’s always stood out to me is how rarely people talk about adopting senior cats or long-term shelter residents, it’s almost always about kittens. I wanted Pawfect Match to change that and give every animal, no matter their age or how long they’ve waited, a real shot at finding a home.

Olivia

I’m Olivia, and I co-created Pawfect Match with Jade to help bring more attention to shelter animals that are too often overlooked, especially those who’ve been waiting the longest. I have three rescue pets of my own: two dogs, Atlas and Chase, and a bunny named Skinny. Each of them came into my life in different ways, but they’ve all taught me how much love rescue animals have to give. It’s heartbreaking how many pets get passed over just because they’re not puppies, kittens, or the “perfect” breed. With Pawfect Match, I wanted to help change that by giving every animal a better chance to be seen and adopted, no matter their story.
